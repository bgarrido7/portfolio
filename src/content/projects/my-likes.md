## About

Have you ever been asked to recommend a movie and suddenly forgotten every single one you’ve ever seen?

Yeah… that happens to me more often than I’d like to admit.

But even when I *do* remember, everything I like is just… scattered. Some things are on one app, others on a different website. Movies in one place, books in another, games somewhere else, music somewhere else again. It never really felt like *my* collection—just pieces of it spread all over the internet.

So I made this.

It’s a simple place where I can store everything I love—books, video games, music, anime—all in one spot. No switching between platforms, no trying to remember where I saved something. Just one app that actually reflects my taste as a whole.

It’s not trying to replace those big, specific platforms. It just connects everything in a way that makes sense for me. I can keep track of what I’ve experienced, organize it however I want, and always have my favorites ready—whether I’m recommending something or just revisiting things I love.

And on a personal note, this project was also my excuse to finally try Vue.js for the first time. It gave me a way to build something meaningful while exploring a new framework and learning as I went.

Basically, it’s my personal media space.

And now you can see it too.
